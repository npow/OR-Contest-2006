What's ACOS?

It is the automation system used in Operation Research Contest (Spring 2006) held by the department of Combinatorics and Optimization, faculty of Mathematics, the University of Waterloo.

This application is unofficially supported by the servers facilities of the University of Waterloo's Computer Science Club (CSC). The framework of Asynchronous JavaScript and XML (AJAX), X-Mode Web Framework "Yume", is originally designed for the beta version of the official website of CSC and Shiroyuki Project and it is a part of X-Mode Project developed by Juti Noppornpitak.
